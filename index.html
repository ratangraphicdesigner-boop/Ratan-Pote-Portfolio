About Me

A designer who believes in the power of visual communication

I'm Ratan, a Mumbai-based graphic designer passionate about creating designs that tell stories and drive results. My journey began in 2011, and since then, I've had the privilege of working with diverse clients across industries.


I specialize in brand identity, marketing collateral, packaging design, and digital content creation. Whether it's a startup looking for their first logo or an established company needing a complete marketing overhaul, I bring the same level of creativity and dedication to every project.
